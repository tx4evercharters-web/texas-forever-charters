Cancellation policies are managed through FareHarbor at the time of booking. We do our best to work with guests in the case of bad weather or unexpected circumstances.

Glass is allowed on the Carver yacht but must stay inside the cabin. Glass is not permitted on the pontoon.
Yes, there are enough life jackets for everyone on board. Children under 13 are required to wear one at all times per Texas lake law.
Smoking is not allowed on either vessel. You're welcome to smoke in the water or on a float. Vaping is allowed on board.
You break it, you buy it. Throwing up in the toilet is a $200 fee, please use the lake or a trash bag instead. No feminine products in the toilets or a $200 fee applies.
No, we do not offer fishing charters.
We are not lifeguards. Guests swim at their own risk.
All charters must be paid in full one week in advance. Deposits are non-refundable within two weeks of the charter date. If you cancel 5 or more days before your charter, we will reimburse 50% of the total. After that, it is non-refundable — unless weather is the reason for cancellation.

On weather: our captains personally monitor all conditions and will never take guests out in a dangerous situation. Lake Travis is unique — even when it's raining in Austin, the rain often bypasses the lake entirely due to local land formations and wind patterns. A high chance of rain in the forecast does not mean your charter will be affected.

Thunderstorms are a different story. We will issue a full refund for any time lost on the water due to thunderstorms.
