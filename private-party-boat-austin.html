Austin's Go-To Private Boat Party

When you want a venue that actually creates a memory, not just a room with a view, a private boat charter on Lake Travis is the answer. Texas Forever Charters has hosted bachelorette parties, milestone birthdays, corporate team outings, graduation celebrations, and everything in between. The lake is always the best part of the story.

The 1996 Carver Aft Cabin motor yacht is built for this. At up to 20 guests, a full interior below deck with salon, kitchen, bedroom, and 2 fully functional restrooms, a 14ft jump, float mats, and a paddle board, there's genuinely no private event venue in Austin that competes with a day on this boat.

And because it's fully captained by DJ or Dane, you don't have to manage anything. You just show up with your people, BYOB, and let Lake Travis do what it does.

What's Included For Private Parties

  • Up to 20 guests on the Carver yacht
  • Licensed captain for the full charter
  • Full interior cabin below deck

Occasions We Love

  • Bachelorette and bachelor parties
  • Birthday celebrations (any age)
  • Corporate team outings and retreats
