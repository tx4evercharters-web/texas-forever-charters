Lake Travis: Austin's Best Day on the Water

Lake Travis stretches nearly 65 miles through the Texas Hill Country northwest of Austin. With deep blue water, dramatic limestone bluffs, and wide-open coves perfect for anchoring, it's one of the best lakes in the state for a real day out on the water.

The lake is big enough to feel like an escape, no shoreline traffic, no crowded swim beaches, but close enough to Austin that you're back in the city by dinner. For locals, it's a weekend ritual. For visitors, it's often the highlight of the trip.

Texas Forever Charters operates out of Volente Beach Waterpark & Resort, one of the most scenic launch points on the lake, right at the edge of some of the best water on Lake Travis.

What to Expect From Your Rental

Every Texas Forever Charter is fully captained, meaning Captain DJ or Dane is with you the entire time. They handle departure, docking, route planning, and everything in between. Your job is to relax and enjoy the lake.

Most charters include a run out to Devil's Cove, the most popular anchoring spot on Lake Travis, where guests hang out on float mats, jump from the platform, and do exactly nothing that resembles work. From there, the route is flexible based on the group's preferences and conditions.

  • Departs from Volente Beach
  • Captain handles all navigation
  • Devil's Cove is a regular stop
  • Float mats, jump, paddle board included
  • BYOB, coolers welcome on board
  • Catering add-on available
  • Drone footage add-on available
  • 2–8 hour charter windows

How to Book a Lake Travis Boat Rental

Booking takes just a few minutes. Choose your date, select the Carver motor yacht (up to 20 guests) or the Bentley Navigator pontoon (up to 13 guests), pick your charter length, and you're set.

We recommend booking at least one to two weeks in advance for summer weekends, Lake Travis is popular from May through September and spots go fast. For weekday or off-peak rentals, same-week booking is often possible.

Questions before booking? Call us directly at (737) 368-1669. We're always happy to talk through what makes sense for your group.
