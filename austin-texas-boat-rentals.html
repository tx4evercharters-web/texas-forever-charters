Why Choose a Captained Boat Rental?

Most boat rentals in Austin send you out on the water alone. You're responsible for navigation, safety, docking, and keeping track of everyone on board. Texas Forever Charters works differently. Every charter comes with a licensed, experienced captain, either DJ or Dane, who takes care of everything from departure to return.

You don't need a boating license, prior experience, or any knowledge of Lake Travis. Your captain knows every cove, current, and sandbar on the lake. All you need is a group of people ready to have a great time.

It's a fundamentally better way to spend a day on the water. You're not renting a boat, you're booking an experience with someone who's been doing this for years and genuinely loves Lake Travis.

What's Included With Every Rental

Texas Forever Charters includes everything you need for a complete day on Lake Travis. There's nothing to pick up at a store beforehand and nothing to figure out when you arrive.

  • Licensed, experienced captain
  • Float mats for anchoring and lounging
  • 14ft jump platform (Carver)
  • Paddle board
  • Ice, water, and towels
  • BYOB, bring your own drinks
  • Departure from Volente Beach
  • Full interior cabin (Carver)
  • 2 fully functional restrooms (Carver)
  • Salon, kitchen & bedroom (Carver)

Optional add-ons include professional drone footage and photos, catering through our partners at Beachside Billies, and more. Just ask at booking.

Two Boats. One Great Experience.

We run two vessels out of Volente Beach Waterpark & Resort on Lake Travis. For larger groups and special occasions, the 1996 Carver Aft Cabin motor yacht handles up to 20 guests and includes a full interior, salon, kitchenette, bedroom, and 2 restrooms. It's one of the few charter boats on Lake Travis with a real cabin below deck.

For smaller groups, couples, and families, the 24ft Bentley Navigator pontoon is the right call. Comfortable, stable, and perfect for sunset cruises, tubing, and casual lake days. Handles up to 13 guests.

Both boats are fully captained by DJ or Dane. Same quality, same service, just a different size and feel depending on what your group needs.
