Charter Agreement

Terms of Service

Texas Forever Charters · Lake Travis · Austin, Texas

Welcome aboard. By booking a charter with Texas Forever Charters, you (the charter organizer) and every guest in your party agree to the following terms. Please read carefully — these terms govern every charter we operate.

Payment Terms

A non-refundable 10% booking fee is due at the time of reservation to secure your charter date. The remaining balance is due in full no later than 14 days before your charter date. If full payment is not received by the 14-day deadline, you will be contacted by the owners. If payment cannot be collected within 24 hours of that contact, your charter will be cancelled and your booking fee will be forfeited.

Damage Deposit

A $250 damage deposit hold is placed on your card at checkout. This is a pre-authorization only — no funds are withdrawn unless damage occurs during your charter. The hold will be released within 48 hours after your charter if no damage is reported. If damage occurs, the actual cost of repair or replacement will be charged to your card. The $250 minimum applies regardless of damage amount.

Gratuity

A minimum gratuity of 20% of the charter total is required and should be paid directly to your captain on the day of the charter, in cash or via Venmo. Gratuity is not included in the charter price.

Cancellation Policy

  • Booking fee (10%): non-refundable under all circumstances.
  • 14+ days before charter: full refund of balance paid.
  • 7–13 days before charter: 50% refund of balance paid.
  • Less than 7 days before charter: no refund.
  • Weather cancellation initiated by Texas Forever Charters: full refund including booking fee, or free reschedule at your choice.
  • Guest-initiated cancellations due to weather concerns: standard cancellation policy applies.

Rescheduling

All rescheduling requests must be discussed directly with the owners by calling or texting (737) 368-1669. Rescheduling is subject to availability and owner approval. Requests made 7 or more days before your charter date will be considered on a case-by-case basis. Requests made within 7 days of your charter date require documented proof of emergency (medical emergency, death in family, etc.) and are at the sole discretion of the owners. Texas Forever Charters is not obligated to reschedule any charter, and rescheduling does not guarantee a refund if unavailable dates cannot be agreed upon.

BYOB Policy

Guests may bring their own alcohol. No glass containers are permitted on deck or on the pontoon — glass is permitted inside the yacht cabin only. The charter organizer is responsible for ensuring no guest under 21 consumes alcohol. Texas Forever Charters reserves the right to terminate the charter without refund if this policy is violated.

Smoking Policy

Smoking anything that produces ash is not permitted on any vessel at any time. Vaping is permitted on deck. Smoking is permitted in the water or on float mats while anchored.

Capacity

Maximum capacity is strictly enforced — 20 guests on the yacht, 13 guests on the pontoon. Children under 13 must wear a USCG-approved life jacket while on deck unless inside the cabin.

Damage Policy

Guests are responsible for the full cost of any damage to the vessel, equipment, or third-party property caused by the charter group. A minimum $250 fee applies. The $250 damage deposit on file will be captured and additional charges applied as needed to cover actual repair or replacement costs.

Liability

Participation in water activities involves inherent risks. By booking a charter, all guests acknowledge and accept these risks. Texas Forever Charters, its owners, captains, and crew are not liable for injury, illness, loss of personal property, or death during the charter. All guests must sign the liability waiver before boarding.

Charter Termination

Texas Forever Charters reserves the right to terminate any charter without refund for guest misconduct, policy violations, unsafe behavior, or weather/water conditions deemed dangerous by the captain. The captain's decision is final.

No Lifeguard

Texas Forever Charters does not provide lifeguard services. Swimming and water activities are at each guest's own risk.

Platform Booking Fee

A non-refundable platform booking fee is charged at checkout. This fee is separate from the charter total and is not refundable under any circumstances.

Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Texas. Disputes will be resolved in Travis County, Texas.

Questions? Call or text the owners directly at (737) 368-1669 or email tx4evercharters@gmail.com. We're happy to walk through anything.
